# Patient Experience Analytics

Repository: `patient-experience-analytics`

## Overview

Survey app for patient satisfaction ratings, NPS analysis, sector breakdowns, manifestations, saved filters, and theme controls.

## Main Capabilities

- Survey form with Excellent, Good, Regular, Poor, and N/A rating blocks.
- Quick period filters, sector and interviewer fields, and saved filters.
- Analytics dashboard with satisfaction, NPS, history chart, donut, and sector table.
- Manifestation list with block-level status management.

## Operating Flow

1. Select the period, sector, and interviewer.
2. Record ratings across the survey blocks.
3. Review satisfaction and NPS analytics.
4. Manage manifestations and saved filters.
